Brand foundation

The dependable infrastructure behind the good work.

TSCP sits between humanitarian mission and hard operational logistics. Everything we do balances those two forces: human warmth on the outside, freight-grade precision underneath. Never one without the other.

Mission

To simplify the supply chain for nonprofits — connecting the organizations that serve people with the logistics capacity, expertise, and goods they need to deliver.

Vision

A world where no mission stalls in transit — with at least 1% of global logistics capacity moving for nonprofits.

Positioning

We match need with capacity and handle the hard middle: shipping, customs, warehousing, last-mile. Humanitarian in purpose, operational in practice.

Impact report · Rwanda

9 million test kits, delivered

One contract, four legs, zero units lost — how a marketplace match became the country's largest malaria-testing resupply.

TSCP coordinated ocean freight into Mombasa, customs clearance, bonded warehousing in Kigali, and last-mile delivery to 412 clinics. The receiving teams ran the show; we carried the boxes.
