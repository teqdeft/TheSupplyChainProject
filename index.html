9 million test kits, delivered

One contract, four legs, zero units lost — how a marketplace match became the country's largest malaria-testing resupply.

TSCP coordinated ocean freight into Mombasa, customs clearance, bonded warehousing in Kigali, and last-mile delivery to 412 clinics. The receiving teams ran the show; we carried the boxes.
