FAQ

Common questions

How much does it cost to register my nonprofit?

Registering is free. TSCP matches your shipment need with donated or discounted capacity from partners; you fund only the freight costs that remain, and gifts to the freight fund help cover those.

How long does registration take?

About five minutes. It's a light vetting step so we can match you with verified capacity. A coordinator replies within two business days.

What can partners contribute?

Carriers can offer transport and backhaul capacity, warehouses can offer storage, technology partners can offer software, and corporations can contribute CSR budget or gifts-in-kind. One registration covers every partner type.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes. The Supply Chain Project is a registered 501(c)(3). You'll receive a tax-deductible receipt by email for every gift.
