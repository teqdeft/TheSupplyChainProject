FAQ

Common questions

How do I join the network?

Select your interest — nonprofit, logistics provider, service provider, technology provider, corporation, student, or volunteer — and create an account. A coordinator will reach out within two business days.

What is the TSCP Marketplace?

An online platform where nonprofits connect with logistics providers, service providers, technology partners, and corporate supporters to fulfill their supply chain needs.

What tools does TSCP offer?

Our platform includes the marketplace, supply chain assessment survey, NOC center for real-time monitoring, ESG reporting, metrics tracking, logistics technology, and map view.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes. The Supply Chain Project is a registered 501(c)(3). You'll receive a tax-deductible receipt by email for every gift.
