What we do

Match need with capacity. Handle the hard middle.

Tell us what you need to move. We find the capacity — and take care of shipping, customs, warehousing, and last-mile, so your team can stay on the mission.

How it works

Every shipment is a path through partners

Step 01 · Posted

You post the need

Origin, destination, what's moving, and by when. Five minutes.

Step 02 · Matched

We match capacity

Vetted carriers, corporate partners, and volunteers who can move it.

Step 03 · In transit

We handle the middle

Shipping, customs, bonded warehousing, last-mile — coordinated end to end.

Step 04 · Delivered

It arrives, confirmed

A documented route and a delivery confirmation. You know where it is, and when it lands.

Services

The whole route, one coordinator

Freight

Multi-modal shipping

Ocean, air, and road — matched to your timeline and budget, with the right mode for each leg.

Compliance

Customs & documentation

Clearance, duties, and paperwork handled and pre-cleared so goods don't sit at the border.

Storage

Bonded warehousing

Secure, compliant storage near the destination — including cold chain when the cargo needs it.

Delivery

Last-mile delivery

The hardest leg. We reach the clinic shelf, the distribution point, the field — not just the port.

Technology

Marketplace & control tower

Post a need, match capacity, and track every shipment through posted, matched, in transit, delivered.

Response

Emergency logistics

Rapid coordination when timing is everything — surge capacity for disaster and crisis response.
