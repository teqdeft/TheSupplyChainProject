Our services

Technology, marketplace, and tools for nonprofit supply chains.

The Supply Chain Project provides an online marketplace with tools and resources to make managing and improving your supply chain accessible and efficient.

Services & Tools

Everything you need in one platform

Marketplace

Online Marketplace

An online platform where nonprofits connect with logistics providers, service providers, and corporate partners to fulfill their supply chain needs.

Technology

Logistics Technology

Smart tools for route optimization, shipment tracking, and supply chain management — built specifically for nonprofit operations.

Assessment

Supply Chain Assessment

Take our free assessment survey to evaluate your nonprofit's supply chain maturity and identify areas for improvement.

Reporting

ESG Reporting & Metrics

Comprehensive environmental, social, and governance reporting. Measurable outcomes for your stakeholders and partners.

Operations

NOC Center & Control Tower

24/7 network operations center for real-time visibility, monitoring, and coordination of your supply chain activities.

Response

Emergency Logistics

Rapid coordination when timing is everything — surge capacity and technology support for disaster and crisis response.

SCP Tools

Intelligent, automated tools for your supply chain.

Our technology platform includes the NOC Center for real-time monitoring, the TSCP Assessment Survey for supply chain evaluation, logistics tech for optimization, and map view for geographic visibility.
